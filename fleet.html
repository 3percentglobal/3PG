01 — The Fleet

The
Fleet

A Federation of Sovereign Commanders

The Alliance

Entry into The Fleet is not applied for. It is recognized.

The Fleet is the inner circle of 3 Percent Global — the commanders without whom the label does not exist. Not artists signed to a deal. Not employees given a title. Sovereign operators who each hold a pillar of the ecosystem: the voice, the sound, the anchor, and the architecture. Together they form something the industry was not built to produce.

001
Rosalvo
Founder · President · Main Artist

Born Fritz-Joël Clerveaux in Montreal, rooted in Gonaïves, Haiti. Rosalvo is the founder of 3 Percent Global, the creator of the Troubatrap and KompaTrap genres, and the main artist of the label. His music is a bridge — between the ancestral rhythms of Haiti and the trap of the American South, between the roots and the streets. Trilingue. Fully sovereign. Unbothered by what the industry expects.

002
Lens Dupuy
Executive Producer · Label Administrator

Born in Montreal, rooted in Gonaïves, Haiti. Lens Dupuy builds both worlds simultaneously — the sonic architecture of Rosalvo’s entire catalogue, and the operational infrastructure of 3PG itself. Co-founder of the TroubaTrap genre. 130M+ streams. 2x Diamond certified (SNEP), including “Vie” with Damso, produced at 19. Dolby Atmos expert. Label administrator. The man behind the machine — in every sense.

003
Jeff
Manager · The Anchor

Jeff is the force that holds the operation together when everything else moves. Not a manager in the traditional sense — someone who shows up when it counts, finances what needs to be financed, and makes sure the people around him are taken care of. In any ecosystem, there is always someone whose value cannot be measured in credits or metrics. Jeff is that person for 3PG.

004
Wvssiii
Composer · Engineer · Digital Builder

Born Wassim Chihabiddine in Moroni, Comoros. Trained across France, Canada, and Réunion. Wvssiii is the technical and creative backbone of 3PG’s digital presence — sound designer, composer, audio engineer, filmmaker, developer, and multimedia architect. If something exists visually or digitally in the 3PG world, he built it. A true multi-disciplinary sovereign operator.
