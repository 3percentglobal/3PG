Who We Are

3 Percent Global is not a record label. It is a Headquarters — a sovereign ecosystem built for those who refused to wait for permission.

Founded in Montreal by Rosalvo and Lens Dupuy — two sons of Gonaïves, Haiti — 3PG was born from a simple and irreversible decision: to build something real, something lasting, on terms that belong to no one but the creators themselves.

The name comes from a simple and uncomfortable observation: in any given field, in any given generation, only 3% of people operate at the level required to build something that lasts. The other 97% are waiting — for the right moment, the right deal, the right sign. The 3% build regardless.

We use music as a lever, not a ceiling. The objective is total freedom — financial, mental, and spiritual. Every project, every partnership, every decision made within 3PG is aligned with this singular mission. Nothing is done by accident.

We are a federation of sovereign commanders. Not employees. Not artists waiting to be discovered. Builders. Architects of a new paradigm. The 3% who understand that true power is built — not given, not borrowed, and not begged for.

“We do not seek validation from the systems we are building to replace.”

“Entry is not offered. It is earned.”

“We do not release music to fill space. We release it when it is ready, when it is true, and when the world is ready to receive it.”

“The 3% do not wait for permission. They build — and one day, what they built becomes undeniable.”

The Four Pillars
— 01

Matrix Mastery

Understanding the systems that govern money, culture, and power — and operating above them. The matrix was not built for us. We build our own. Contracts, rights, distribution, publishing — not obstacles but instruments of sovereignty.

— 02

Financial Sovereignty

Every master owned. Every deal built on equity, not exploitation. Generational wealth is not a byproduct — it is the objective. We build for 50 years from now. Every revenue stream structured with permanence in mind.

— 03

Spiritual Alignment

We do not manufacture. We transmit. Every release carries an undeniable truth rooted in who we are and where we come from — Gonaïves, Moroni, Montreal. Spirit before strategy. Always.

— 04

Knowledge Transmission

The Headquarters does not hoard knowledge. What we learn, we pass forward. The tools of liberation are shared within the alliance — city to city, generation to generation. The elevation of one is the elevation of all.
