The Root System

We did not arrive here by accident. We are the continuation of something ancient — something that was never given permission to exist, and existed anyway.

3 Percent Global is built on the understanding that we carry more than our own history. Rosalvo and Lens Dupuy were born in Montreal but rooted in Gonaïves, Haiti. Wvssiii was born in Moroni, Comoros. The cities are different. The roots are the same — a tradition of building without permission, of carrying culture across oceans and into rooms that were never designed to receive it.

Heritage, for us, is not nostalgia. It is infrastructure. Understanding where we come from — culturally, spiritually, and historically — is what makes every decision we make more intentional. The twoubadou, the konpa, the sounds of the Caribbean and the Indian Ocean — these are not just influences. They are the foundation.

Troubatrap was not created in a vacuum. It was created by people who understood exactly where they came from and refused to leave it behind when they entered new territory. That is the heritage of 3PG.

“They tried to bury us. They did not know we were seeds.”

— Dinos Christianopoulos

“Bridging Roots and Streets.”

— Rosalvo

This section is dedicated to the root system. To the artists, thinkers, and ancestors whose work made our work possible. To the cultural lineage — Haitian, Comorian, Caribbean, African — that we carry and are responsible to continue. To the knowledge that was passed down so we could pass it forward.

— Haitian Roots

Gonaïves — The City of Independence

Rosalvo and Lens Dupuy are rooted in Gonaïves — the city where Haiti declared its independence in 1804. The first Black republic in the world. The 3PG ethos of sovereignty is not abstract. It is ancestral.

— Comorian Roots

Moroni — The Island Frequency

Wvssiii was born in Moroni, Comoros — a small island nation in the Indian Ocean with a vast cultural heritage. His presence in 3PG is a signal that this ecosystem is not limited by geography. The frequency travels.

— TroubaTrap Origins

A Genre Born from Memory

TroubaTrap was created by fusing the twoubadou — the traditional Haitian troubadour tradition — with modern trap. It is not a gimmick. It is a cultural act of preservation and evolution, carried out in real time, with full respect for what came before.

— Forward Transmission

What We Owe the Next Generation

Heritage is not just about looking back. Every module in The Vault, every podcast episode, every piece of knowledge transmitted through 3PG is a letter to someone who is not born yet. We take that responsibility seriously.

Sovereignty
3 Percent Global Inc. — Core Principle

Sovereignty is not a word we use lightly. It is the foundation on which every decision at 3 Percent Global is made. Every master owned. Every contract built on equity. Every deal structured to serve the creator — not extract from them.

Master Ownership

Every artist signed to 3PG retains full ownership of their master recordings. The label holds the rights corporately — but the artist, as founder or signee, benefits directly from the value those masters generate. No external party owns what we build.

Publishing Control

3 Percent Global is a registered publisher with SOCAN. The 50% publisher share on every composition stays within the ecosystem — not in the black boxes of intermediaries. Every stream, every radio spin, every sync generates revenue that comes back to the creators.

The 3% Standard

Every partnership, every deal, every collaboration is evaluated against one question: does this serve the long-term sovereignty of the creators involved? If the answer is no — we don't do it.

Publishing
3 Percent Global Inc.

Every composition created within the 3PG ecosystem is protected, registered, and monetized globally. We do not leave money on the table — and we do not leave our creators unprotected.

What This Means

Every time a 3PG composition plays anywhere in the world — on a stream, on the radio, in a film, in a venue — the publishing revenue comes back to the ecosystem. Not to intermediaries. Not to third parties. Here.

Our Model

Creators keep what they create. The label administers — it does not own. No songwriter ever transfers ownership of their compositions to 3 Percent Global. We collect on your behalf. You keep your work.

Global, Without Exception

The 3PG catalogue is registered and protected in 210+ countries. Every market. Every platform. Every use. If someone uses the music, the ecosystem gets paid.
