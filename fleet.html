01 — The Fleet

The
Fleet

A Federation of Sovereign Commanders

The Alliance

Entry into The Fleet is not applied for. It is recognized.

The Fleet is the inner circle of 3 Percent Global — the commanders without whom the label does not exist. Not artists signed to a deal. Not employees given a title. Sovereign operators who each hold a pillar of the ecosystem: the voice, the sound, the anchor, and the architecture. Together they form something the industry was not built to produce.

001
Rosalvo
Founder · Chief Architect · Main Artist

Born Fritz-Joël Clerveaux in Montreal, rooted in Gonaïves, Haiti. Rosalvo is the founder of 3 Percent Global, the founder of the TroubaTrap and KonpaTrap genres, and the main artist of the label. His music is a bridge — between the ancestral rhythms of Haiti and the trap of the American South, between the roots and the streets. Trilingue. Fully sovereign. Unbothered by what the industry expects.

002
Lens Dupuy
Executive Producer · Label Administrator

Born in Montreal, rooted in Gonaïves, Haiti. Lens Dupuy is the founder of TroubaTrap & KonpaTrap and the Executive Producer and Administrator of 3 Percent Global. He is the architect of Rosalvo’s entire sonic universe — every composition, every sound, every world built from the ground up. Without the architecture, there is no movement.

003
Jeff
Manager · The Anchor

Manager of 3 Percent Global. Jeff operates with the kind of commitment that does not require a title to be felt. He is the steady presence that keeps the operation moving — focused, reliable, and fully aligned with the mission.

004
Wvssiii
Composer · Engineer · Digital Builder

Born Wassim Chihabiddine in Moroni, Comoros. Trained across France, Canada, and Réunion. Wvssiii is a composer, audio engineer, developer, and multimedia creator. Sound design, visual production, code, camera — he operates across disciplines with the same precision and intention that defines everything within 3PG.

005
Annaicart Mazile
Creative Director · Image Architect

Creative Director of 3 Percent Global. Annaicart Mazile architects the visual identity of the label — managing the image of Rosalvo, Lens Dupuy, Wvssiii, and the entire ecosystem. Everything visible is as intentional as everything audible. Host of Limen Limyè, a podcast dedicated to Haitian culture and education. TikToker recognized in Haïti. Présidente of adndphaiti.com.
