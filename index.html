3 Percent Global is not a record label. It is a Global Sovereignty Infrastructure — a Command Node built for those who refused to wait for permission.

Founded in Montreal by Rosalvo — 3PG was born from a simple and irreversible decision: to build something real, something lasting, on terms that belong to no one but the creators themselves.
