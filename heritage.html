The Origin

3 Percent Global was not built in a boardroom. It was built in the mind of two kids from Montreal who understood something most people never will — that the system was not designed for them, and that was not a problem. It was a signal.

It started around 2013–2014. Fritz-Joël Clerveaux and a childhood friend had a concept taking shape — something they called the 3 Percent. Not a company. Not a label. A way of seeing the world. They moved with it through high school, started a movement, and watched others follow. Two young people with a vision that was bigger than anything around them could contain.

The concept was simple and total: understand the matrix you operate in. Know who you are. Constantly improve yourself. Build financial freedom — not as a goal, but as a foundation. Share your resources. Transmit your knowledge. Lift the people around you. Stay aligned with something greater than the material. Be your own boss. Never stop learning. Never stop growing. Never stop trying to understand the universe you live in.

“97% of people are waiting. For permission. For the right moment. For someone to open the door. The 3% build the door themselves — and then they leave it open for the next person.”

— Fritz-Joël Clerveaux, Founder

What started as a movement between two friends became a structure. Fritz-Joël took the concept further, deeper, more concrete — and over years of deliberate construction, 3 Percent Global became what it is today: an infrastructure built entirely outside the systems most people take for granted.

Not a label that signs artists and takes their masters. Not a company that plays by rules written by people who never had their interests in mind. An ecosystem — for music, for real estate, for finance, for every domain where sovereign operators need a structure that matches their level.

The Three Pillars
— 01

Music & Culture

The first pillar and the most visible. 3PG operates as a full-stack music infrastructure — masters owned, publishing controlled, distribution sovereign. Not a label that extracts. A structure that amplifies. Built for indie artists and labels who want the power of a major without surrendering what they built.

— 02

Real Estate & Capital

Wealth built in music must be converted into something permanent. Real estate is one of the oldest forms of sovereignty. 3PG is building the infrastructure for aligned operators to access, understand, and move in this space — without gatekeepers, without the usual barriers designed to keep certain people out.

— 03

Knowledge & Transmission

The third pillar is the one that multiplies everything else. What 3PG learns, it transmits. The Vault is the beginning. The mission is to build a living body of knowledge — about money, about rights, about systems, about self — that reaches the people who need it most and changes what they believe is possible.

— The Standard

Built from the Root. Open to the World.

3 Percent Global was built with a specific depth — and that depth is what makes it strong enough to hold anyone. The only criteria for alignment is the decision to operate outside the 97%. Where you come from is your power. What you build with it is your legacy.
