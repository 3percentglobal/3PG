Access Requires
Alignment

The infrastructure of 3 Percent Global is not a public offering. Entry into the ecosystem — whether as a creator, a partner, a brand, or a collaborator — requires demonstrated alignment with our principles, our standards, and our mission.

We do not respond to cold pitches. We do not sign artists because of their follower count. We align with operators — people who have already demonstrated, through their actions and their work, that they operate at the required frequency.

What Happens After You Submit
01
Your Submission Is Read

Every submission is read by a member of the 3PG team. Not an algorithm. A person. We take this seriously.

02
Initial Evaluation

We evaluate alignment — not talent, not follower count. We are looking for evidence of a specific mindset: someone who builds deliberately and owns what they create.

03
Response or Silence

If your submission demonstrates alignment, you will receive a response within 5–10 business days. No response within 14 days means not aligned at this time. Resubmit in 90 days.

04
The Conversation

If we respond, the next step is a conversation — not a pitch meeting. We want to understand who you are and how 3PG can serve your mission.

Sovereignty
3 Percent Global Inc.

Sovereignty is not a word we use lightly. It is the foundation on which every decision at 3 Percent Global is made. Every master owned. Every contract built on equity. Every deal structured to serve the creator — not extract from them.

Master Ownership

Every artist within the 3PG ecosystem owns what they create. This is not a courtesy — it is a structural requirement. No external party holds rights over what is built here.

No Permission Required

3PG was built to eliminate the need to ask for permission — from labels, from systems, from anyone. The infrastructure exists so that creators can operate at the highest level on their own terms.

The 3% Standard

Every partnership, every deal, every collaboration is evaluated against one question: does this serve the long-term sovereignty of the creators involved? If the answer is no — we don't do it.

Publishing
3 Percent Global Inc.

Every composition created within the 3PG ecosystem is protected, registered, and monetized globally. We do not leave money on the table — and we do not leave our creators unprotected.

What This Means

Every time a 3PG composition plays anywhere in the world — on a stream, on the radio, in a film, in a venue — the publishing revenue comes back to the ecosystem. Not to intermediaries. Not to third parties. Here.

Our Model

Creators keep what they create. The label administers — it does not own. No songwriter ever transfers ownership of their compositions to 3 Percent Global. We collect on your behalf. You keep your work.

Global, Without Exception

The 3PG catalogue is registered and protected in 210+ countries. Every market. Every platform. Every use. If someone uses the music, the ecosystem gets paid.

Licensing
3 Percent Global Inc. — All Rights Reserved

All music, audio recordings, visual content, brand assets, and intellectual property associated with 3 Percent Global Inc. and its artists are fully protected. No content may be used, reproduced, or distributed without explicit written authorization.

Sync Licensing

We actively pursue sync placements for the 3PG catalogue in film, television, advertising, and interactive media. All sync requests are reviewed and negotiated directly by the label. Submit requests to licensing@3percent.global.

Master Use Licenses

Any use of a master recording from the 3PG catalogue requires a master use license. Submit all requests with project description, intended use, territory, duration, and budget.

Sampling & Interpolation

Unauthorized sampling or interpolation of any 3PG catalogue is strictly prohibited. All clearance requests must be submitted in writing before any recording begins.
